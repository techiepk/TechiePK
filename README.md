# Parth Kulkarni

Product designer at **[72F Studio](https://github.com/72f-studio)** — I design systems, not just screens.

I believe the best interfaces disappear into the experience. Getting there starts with understanding who something is actually for — their explicit needs and the implicit ones they haven't named yet.

---

### What I'm building

**Penpot Design System Generator** — a plugin that generates a complete design system in 2 interactions. Fonts, colors, shadows, radii, semantic tokens, light and dark modes, across 12+ presets including HIG, MD3, and Polaris. Built on Penpot 2.14's native token API. Open source.

**Desi Data** *(in progress)* — an Indian locale data plugin for Penpot. Real names, addresses, phone numbers, UPI IDs, and regional content across Indian languages. Because lorem ipsum doesn't cut it for products built for Indian users.

**72F Swatch** *(in progress)* — a registry for DESIGN.md design system files with visual previews. Think npm, but for design foundations.

---

### Background

3 years in AAA game production at Ubisoft — feature owner for cinematics, top 10 bug reporter in a team of 70+. That QA background gave me an instinct for edge cases and a methodology for building things that don't break.

The design system generator is V2. V1 failed after 12 large revisions with no clean way to isolate what broke. V2 was built unit by unit — 224 small, testable increments. Shipped cleanly. The QA years made that approach instinctive.
